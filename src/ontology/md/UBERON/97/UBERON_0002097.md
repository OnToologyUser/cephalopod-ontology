
## Class : skin of body

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002097
 * *Label* = skin of body
 * *Definition* = Nonparenchymatous organ that consists of the dermis and epidermis. Subdivisions of the skin surround various body parts; as a whole, the skin constitutes the external layer of the body. Examples: There is only one skin. // Surface structure that consists of the external membranous integument of the animal.
