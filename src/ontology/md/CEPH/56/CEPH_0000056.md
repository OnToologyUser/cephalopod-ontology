
## Class : cephalic cartilage

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/CEPH_0000056
 * *Label* = cephalic cartilage
 * *Definition* = Cartilage surrounding the posterior part of the brain of cephalopods and generally encompasing the statocysts. Anteriorly the cartilage thins into facia making a well-defined limit difficult to establish. The cartilage has a large central foramen through which the esophagus passes and minor foramina for nerves and blood vessels.
